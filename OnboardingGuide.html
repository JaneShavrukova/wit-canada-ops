New member onboarding — Lead guide
Everything a Lead needs to do — from first row to handing off to Ops.
Add the member ▼
1
Add a new row in WIT_Members
First name · Last name · Role · Location · Personal email
2
Send the contract
Send manually, then set Contract Status = sent.
3
Member signs → upload & confirm
Upload the signed contract to the designated Drive folder. Set Contract Status = signed.
Request email & access ▼
4
Request WIT email auto
Set Email Status = requested.

The system queues the member for the Monday batch email sent to Admin (Global Team).
Contract must be signed and uploaded before requesting the email. The script will block you if it isn't.
5
Select groups & request access
Check all required Google Groups columns based on the member's role and region. Then set Add to Groups = requested.

This is where your work ends — Operations takes over from here.
What happens automatically: Every Monday at 7 AM, a batch email is sent to Admin with all pending requests. Status flips requested → sent automatically after the email sends.
Ops takes over from here — assigns group access, checks Intro Sent in the sheet and the onboarding welcome email sends automatically. auto
Tracks completion and sets Member Status = active.
Definition of done ▼

A member is fully onboarded when all of these are true:

✓ Contract signed & uploaded
✓ WIT email active
✓ Added to Google Groups
✓ Drive & Google Chat access granted
✓ Onboarding checklist complete
Member Status = active — set by Ops when all steps are done.
Contract Status ▼
not sent Default. No contract action yet.
sent Contract sent to the member. Waiting for signature.
signed Signed contract uploaded to Drive. Member is confirmed.
Email Status ▼

The Lead sets requested. Ops and Admin update the rest.

— Default. No action taken yet.
requested Lead sets this. Queues the member for the Monday batch email to Admin.
sent Batch email sent to Admin. Waiting for WIT account creation.
created WIT email created. Ops will assign group access.
not activated Email created but activation pending. Follow-up required.
active Member activated their WIT email. Setup complete.
Add to Groups ▼

The Lead sets requested. Ops updates the rest.

— Default. No action taken yet.
requested Lead ticked the required groups and set this status. Ops will assign access.
added Member added to all required Google Groups. Drive and Chat access granted automatically.
Member Status ▼
— Default. Member added but onboarding not yet started.
onboarding Onboarding instructions sent by Ops. Member is working through the checklist.
active Fully onboarded. Set by Ops when all steps are complete.
Photos & bios sync only runs for rows where Member Status is active or onboarding.
Lead▼
Adds the member to the sheet
Sends and tracks the contract
Requests WIT email by setting status to requested
Ticks the required Google Groups columns
Sets Add to Groups = requested after ticking the required group columns
Operations (Ops Lead) ▼
Coordinates WIT email creation with Admin
Grants access: Google Groups, Drive, Google Chat
Checks Intro Sent in the sheet — onboarding welcome email sends automatically auto
Tracks onboarding completion — sets Member Status = active
Admin (Global Team) ▼
Creates the @women-in-tech.org email account in Google Admin
Notified via the automated Monday batch email sent by the system.
Member ▼
Signs the contract
Activates their WIT email
Completes the onboarding checklist independently
