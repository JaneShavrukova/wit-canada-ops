What is this file ▼

The central member roster for WIT Canada. Tracks everyone on the team — their info, onboarding progress, Google Group memberships, and tool access.

Working sheets
WIT_Members
Main roster — internal Canada team. One row per member. All onboarding tracking lives here.
WIT_External
Global WIT members with access to specific Canadian groups. No onboarding flow — group access tracking only.
Auto-generated report sheets
Report_Groups
Snapshot of every Google Group and its members. Rebuilt on demand — never edit manually.
Report_Email_Requests
Members with a pending WIT email creation request (status: requested, sent, or created).
Report_Photos&Bios
Members missing a profile photo or bio in the shared Drive folders, grouped by region.
The 2025 tab is archived — historical data only.
Who does what ▼
L
Team Lead
Adds new member to the sheet, ticks Google Groups, sets Email Status to requested.
O
Ops Lead
Assigns group access, checks Intro Sent — onboarding welcome email sends automatically. Tracks completion and sets Member Status = active.

Report_Groups sheet ▼

A read-only snapshot of every Google Group — who's in it and their role. Pulls data from both WIT_Members and WIT_External.

Members from WIT_External appear in italic grey to distinguish them from internal members.

Rows are collapsible — use the group indicators on the left to fold and unfold each list.

Refresh via WIT Operations → Refresh groups snapshot. The sheet is deleted and recreated each time — never edit it manually.

How group access works ▼

Google Groups are how we control access. Being in a group gives the member email, Shared Drive access, and Google Chat membership — all from one place.

Groups are also used for email distribution. When an email is sent to a group address, all members of that group receive the message.

Canada Team
Every internal member. Gives access to the WIT Canada General Shared Drive.
Leadership
Leads and heads. Gives access to the Leadership Shared Drive.
All other groups
Topic or region-based. Access scoped to that team's resources.
Always use Groups to grant access — never add someone to Drive or Chat directly.

External members ▼

Global WIT members with access to specific Canadian groups — usually via NA Awards or partnerships. Tracked in WIT_External.

No onboarding flow. Manage their group access the same way — tick the columns, then run the sync.

These are the actions in the WIT Operations menu at the top of the screen.
